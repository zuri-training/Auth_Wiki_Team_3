About us

Auth Wiki is a library of authentication codes where users are able to view and download authentication codes. The platform gives it’s users access to view example usage and an online community where they can interact.

Despite being new in the industry, Auth WIki has made it’s platform to be free, fast and fun with amazing and easy to use features.
